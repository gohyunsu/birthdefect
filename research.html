SELSA

Sequence-level similarity aggregation

Motivates aggregating semantically similar frames across the full video sequence rather than only using adjacent frames. It should be compared against single-frame and temporal-pooling baselines.

SAD

Semi-supervised anomaly detection on dynamic graphs

Motivates memory-bank and pseudo-label contrastive learning when CHD labels are scarce. A medical adaptation must make graph nodes/edges explicit and audit pseudo-label errors.
